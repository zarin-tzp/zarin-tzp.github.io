Colorful Salad

Ingredients:

  • Mixed Greens - 2 cups.
  • Chicken - Rotisserie, 1/2 cup, chopped.
  • Chickpeas or Garbanzo beans -1/2 cup, drained from can.
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 1/2 cup, halved.
  • Snacking Cucumbers - 2, sliced.
  • Sweet Peppers -3/4 cup, chopped.
  • Red Onion - 1/4 cup, chopped.
  • Avocado - 1/2, chopped.
  • Artichoke Hearts - 1/2 cup, quartered.
  • Fetta - 1/4 cup, crumbled.
  • Cilantro - to taste, chopped.

Simple Dressing:

  • Lemon - 1/2 or to taste, juiced.
  • Olive Oil - to taste..
  • Sea Salt - to taste..

Prep Time:
5 mins

Cook Time:
None

Total Time:
5 mins

Servings
2

Instructions

In a serving dish, lay the mixed greens in a flat layer.
Arrange the remaining salad ingredients on top of the greens in sections as desired.
Top off with chopped cilantro and dressing ingredients.

Enjoy your delicious and healthy colorful salad!
